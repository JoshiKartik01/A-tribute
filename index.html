Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician. He played a pivotal role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests and led several major projects in ISRO and DRDO. He was deeply loved for his humility and contribution to education and youth empowerment. His vision for a developed India continues to inspire millions.
